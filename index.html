Movie Sync

Movie Sync is a movie discovery app that lets you swipe and match with your friends similar to Tinder!

It received nearly 30k new users on launch day.

The whole app started as a random designer's post from Reddit. I fell in love with the idea and the design immediately upon seeing it. With a couple of quick DMs, we quickly found a common interest, and the 2-man crew was formed.

The app needs to perform real-time match checking server-side. And a front-end deck component that needs to handle drag, snap-back, unload and reload cards.

The front-end is built with React and styled-components while the back-end with Node and Firestore.

Tried and True is a recipe management app that aims to bring simplicity to your meal planning and recipe management workflow.

After the brief success of Movie-Sync, I started working on something more functional.

Tried and True must be able to parse ingredient strings into meaningful data, generate weekly meal planners, and handle shopping lists.

I used Hasura to bootstrap the backend and extended its functionality with an ASP.NET web app. There are 2 separate dockerized microservice APIs that can handle parsing ingredients and importing recipes.

I used GraphQL and PostgresSQL for the strictly defined schemas and with the help of Apollo client's caching capabilities, the client UX is highly responsive.
